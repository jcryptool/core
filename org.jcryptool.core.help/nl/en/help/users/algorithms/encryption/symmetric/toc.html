Modern symmetric encryption algorithms can be divided in block ciphers and stream ciphers:

  • Block ciphers encrypt blocks of fixed length (e.g. 64 or 128 bit). Samples of such ciphers are IDEA, RC2, DES, Triple DES, Rijndael (AES), MARS, RC6, Serpent, Twofish, and Threefish.
  • Stream ciphers encrypt messages bit by bit. In this category fall RC4, LFSR, and Dragon.
